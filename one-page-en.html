-Ultrasonic Testing, UT
-Liquid Penetrant Testing, PT
-Magnetic Particle Testing, MT
-Visual Testing, VT
-Radiograph Testing, RT
-InfraRed Thermography, IRT…etc.

About Us

China steel Corporation (CSC) Non-destructive inspection unit established in 1979. Focus on the application and development of non-destructive testing technology. We provide NDT services for company groups and external parties, such as equipment NDT testing, product inspection and NDT technical certify counseling.
CSC Non-destructive inspection unit is a ISO 17025 NDT inspection laboratory (Accreditation Number:0083) that is certificate by TAF (Taiwan Accreditation Foundation) in NDT testing field. We are accept by TAF surveillance and renewal accreditation for quality management and improvement, and provide fair, reliable, fast and accurate service to ensure compliance with regulations and customer requirements.
